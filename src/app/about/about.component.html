About Litas

▻ Litas is a peer-to-peer Internet currency that enables instant, near-zero cost payments to anyone in the world.

▻ Litas is an open source, global payment network that is fully decentralized without any central authorities.

▻ Mathematics secures the network and empowers individuals to control their own finances.

▻ Litas features faster transaction confirmation times and improved storage efficiency than the leading math-based currency.
